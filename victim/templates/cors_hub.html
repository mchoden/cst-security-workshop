🌐 CORS Misconfigurations

Use the Attacker Site (port 5001) or curl to steal data from these misconfigured APIs.

Level 1

Wildcard Origin

API: /cors/api/public-profile

Easy
Hint

Just fetch() it from any origin. Access-Control-Allow-Origin: * means everyone.

Level 2

Regex Bypass

API: /cors/api/trusted-profile — trusts origins ending in trusted-app.com

Medium
Hint

curl -H "Origin: https://evil-trusted-app.com" to bypass the endswith() check.

Level 3

Reflected Origin + Creds

API: /cors/api/secret-profile — needs cookie session_id=valid-session-token

Medium
Hint

Set the cookie on the victim domain, then fetch with credentials:'include' from attacker site.
